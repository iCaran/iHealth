iHealth Covid-19 Isolation Care Program

Author - iHealth
04 January. 6 min read

– Stay home and recover with Apollo Clinics

The team at CRPF hospital durgapur, in partnership with B.C. roy engineering college medical unit, has been working round the clock to ensure that our healthcare services continue uninterrupted. Right from home collection of samples, drive-through RT PCR tests, accurate diagnostic results, online consultations to counselling and creating Covid-safe spaces for patients, the CRPF and BCR groups have pulled all stops to make sure that citizens are looked after in the best possible way.

This consists for those who have tested positive, and will provide 24/7 support for patients for 14 days.

There is no need to panic while dealing with the virus, because there is access to an emergency doctor between 8 p.m and 8 a.m.

These COVID-19 Isolation Care program are designed to assist people with the correct diagnosis and prescribe case-specific appropriate treatment. We take utmost care while attending to every single person, so that they can have a swift and easy recovery, with constant medical attention while staying home.
